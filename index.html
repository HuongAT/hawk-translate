— What Hawk does

Six small superpowers for the curious reader.

A compact bubble next to your selection. A speaker on both sides. A panel that reveals the whole word family. Tools that get out of your way until you need them.

FEATURE / 01
✦

Select & translate

Highlight text on any website. A compact bubble shows the translation right next to your selection — no tab switching.

FEATURE / 02
🔊

Listen, twice

Click the speaker to hear the original or the translation in a natural voice. Click twice for a slower repeat — perfect for pronunciation practice.

FEATURE / 03
⌘

Word families

Select a single word and Hawk shows its part of speech and synonyms — see the whole vocabulary at a glance, not a flat translation.

FEATURE / 04
▣

Full-text popup

Click the toolbar icon to type or paste any text, choose a target language, and translate longer passages instantly.

FEATURE / 05
↗

Right-click menu

Translate any selection with a right-click — no mouse travel to the toolbar, no keyboard shortcuts to memorize.

FEATURE / 06
◐

Tuned to taste

Auto / Light / Dark themes. Classic or Card bubble styles. Auto-close from 0–120 seconds. The bubble shows when you want it — never when you don't.

— Translation backends

Five engines. Zero lock-in.

Works out of the box with no key required. Bring your own free key for the heavy hitters. Pick your preferred service in Options — and if one fails, an inline chooser swaps to another in a click.

Google
Free · Default
Fast, high-quality translations. The default, no key required.
MyMemory
Free · No key
Open backend with around 5,000 words per day, no setup needed.
DeepL
Bring your key
Free tier offers 500k characters per month with your own API key.
Azure
Bring your key
Microsoft Translator. Free tier offers 2M characters per month.
LibreTranslate
Self-hosted
Open-source. Configure your own endpoint — your data, your rules.
↻

One backend down? Switch in a click.

If your chosen backend fails, an inline "Try with…" chooser appears right inside the bubble. No settings trip required.

— Privacy by design

Your reading is nobody's business but yours.

Hawk Translate doesn't collect or transmit any personal data to the developer. The text you translate is sent only to the provider you chose. API keys you enter are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted anywhere except to that provider's own servers.
